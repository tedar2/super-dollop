Books to check out

Every now and then we come across the books that attract our attention. Below it is possible to find a few examples for the evening reading time.

Excerpt From: Charles Duhigg. “The power of Habit”. Apple Books.

Charles Duhigg demonstrates that it is possible to transform our lives via changing and creating habits.

Excerpt From: Jordan B Peterson. “12 rules for life”. Apple Books.

"Humorous, surprising, and informative, Dr. Peterson tells us why skateboarding boys and girls must be left alone, what terrible fate awaits those who criticize too easily, and why you should always pet a cat when you meet one on the street."(Goodreads, 2018)

Excerpt From: Peter Høeg. “Smilla's sense of snow”. Apple Books.

While Smilla Jaspersen thinks more highly of snow and ice than anything else, she is convinced that there happened a terrible crime in a small town.

Excerpt From: Fredrik Backman. “Bear Town”. Apple Books.

People say Beartown is finished, but there are few believers with burning hearts who believe that the Beartown hockey team actually have a shot at winning national semi-finals. All the hopes and dreams are laying as a heavy burden on teenage boys.
